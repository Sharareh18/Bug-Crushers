    • Tips for Success

    Change your shoes

    • Avoid walking in shoes that create tension on your joints
    • Don't wear shoes that are too tight or loose
    • Don't forget to wear clean socks!

    Stay Hydrated

    • Avoid drinking alcohol and soda before or after your walk.
    • Drink plenty of water throughout the day
    • Avoid drinking too much water before bed

    Wear the right clothes

    • Don't wear clothes that are too tight or loose
    • Don't wear clothes that restrict airflow or movement
    • Consider bringing a change of comfortable or clean clothes
